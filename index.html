Jekk Tales

Jekk slowly roams around an empty field as one of his activities.


Jekk stumbles on a rock while he was exploring.


Jekk destroys rock as because it was imperfect to exist.


After Jekk destroys the imperfect rock, he continues to roam around the vast empty field when he stumbles a mysterious stranger.


The stranger asks Jekk, "Do you want a rock?". Jekk is filled with hatred because the rock the stranger was holding was imperfect as well


Jekk proceeds to eat the stranger as the stranger shows him the rock that Jekk thinks, "Imperfect is a sin". The End.
